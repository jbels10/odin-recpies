OG Dirty Soda Recipe

OG Dirty Soda

The original dirty soda recipe calls for Diet Coke, coconut syrup, fresh lime juice, and cream. A refreshing drink that is reminiscent of a soda float.

Ingredients

  • 7.5 ounces Diet or Regular Coke
  • 1.5 tsp fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp coconut syrup
  • 1-2 tbsp half-and-half

Instructions

  1. Fill a glass with ice and pour in soda
  2. Stir in syrup
  3. Add cream
  4. Enjoy!
